About

So what is this thing? Greentwip is the name we use for porting and developing video games. Greentwip is created with the hope for games to become more cross platform and retrieve back old memories for classical games. We're doing our best effort to bring new games into our portfolio, rest asured the wait will not be in vain!

Well, if any of the topics seem interesting to you, have a look at the portfolio section. It might be you find something interesting specially for that big one called Sonic 2 HD. if you have any questions (or just feel like saying hello), either shoot an email to development@greentwip.xyz or stop by @Greentwip on Twitter!

Sonic 2 HD

The development of the highgly acclaimed Sonic 2 HD project has been a challenge, from specification to full implementation, we coded the player physics and various gameplay aspects, tons of bugfixing and are currently working on the Aquatic Ruin Zone / Casino Night Zone / Super Sonic.

Uno & Friends

UNO™ & Friends takes back old memories from the corporation work done at Gameloft (Ubisoft's direct child company for mobile games), while our contribution was only set for Sony Xperia devices (we coded the graphical selection movement), we also contributed with the main developers for support on specific bugs.

Real Soccer/Football 2013

Real Soccer/Football 2013 has been and it will be our main starting point, all related to this game introduced us to the core Windows technologies for modern devices (we're talking UWP by the moment) and so forth, we coded many bugfixes for the audio engine, solved display problems for both Windows and Blackberry platforms, this project took around 1 year to complete while working with Gameloft.

N.O.V.A. 3

Near Orbit Vanguard Alliance 3 was a tricky one, for weeks we ran into one single problem: The game rebooted after purchasing items from the store. Thriving a 3 days run-up without sleeping (and a lot of coffee), we finally managed to squash the bug for the Windows Phone platform.

Pingus

Pingus, the cutest of them all, we ported the game completely for Windows 8.1 and up, altough it was challenging to compile all the necessary, we finally created a release that was nice to play and gave it a little of promotion across Internet.
